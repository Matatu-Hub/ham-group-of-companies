Sentongo Yassin

Group Chief Marketing and Sales Officer

Mr. Yassin Sentongo is the Chief Marketing and Sales Officer of the Ham Group of Companies and an accredited business magnate with experience from an array of backgrounds ranging from marketing, client relations and sales.


Education

Mr. Yassin holds a Bachelors of Business Administration from Makerere University Kampala and is a strong believer in innovation and leveraging of technology as key drivers of business especially with the ever changing business trends in the 21st Century.

Career Highlights

He previously worked at Mavid Pharmacy as a Marketing Officer, then a Senior Marketing Officer at Dynapharm Uganda and as a relationship Manager at Orient Bank Limited before becoming the Group ºs CMO.
