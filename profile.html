Who we are

Ham Group of Companies is a multi-national business conglomerate operating in real estate, manufacturing, hospitality, education, transport, publications and general merchandise.


We have invested more than one hundred million dollars in the sector with developments in residential housing, commercial buildings, industrial facilities and will continue to support the countryʼs growth in providing universal affordable real estate solutions.


Ham Agro-Processing Industries will comprise of ten Integrated Agro-processing Industrial Parks (IAIPs) within the crop and livestock production zone locations in Uganda to facilitate value addition, provide market for locally grown raw materials and encourage commercialization of the sector while increasing exports.


Education is the only guarantee we have for continuous development as a nation and our commitment to this purpose underscored the investment into world class education provided by Hana International School.


Located at 375 Moston Lane M40 9NB Manchester UK, Ham International UK owns rentable property in Manchester. As a fully registered entity in the UK, the property is home to a supermarket on the ground level and residential flats that are all fully let out.
