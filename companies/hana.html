Hana International School

Portfolio
September 07, 2019 12:43 pm

Hana International School was established in 2003 as premium, Ministry of Education accredited, duo-curriculum International School in Kampala, Uganda

Currently, the school has enrolled more than 1000 boarding and day students on its academic program drawn from more than 5 countries.

The school offers Cambridge International Examinations (CIE) curriculum at junior, IGCSE and GCE at O and A Levels. It has both UNEB and Cambridge curricula, and offers O level and A level, fully boarding school.

With over 10 years of service, the school continuously looks at how they can advance their curriculum to provide a relevant and dynamic education program.

The staff is diverse international, fully trained and dedicated to the education of young people. The alumni represent a vast array of professionals, entrepreneurs and public servants.

The school is on a 10-acre piece of land. Management has ensured that the students have access to all facilities by providing for them.

The school has four blocks providing accommodation for the students, five blocks for classrooms and state-of-the art laboratories to cater for all the students º learning requirements.
